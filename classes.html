Class Descriptions

  • HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

    HIIT is a workout that alternates between intense bursts of activity and fixed periods of less-intense activity, or even complete rest. It's a great way to burn calories and build muscle in a short amount of time.

  • Yoga

    Yoga is a group of physical, mental, and spiritual practices or disciplines which originated in ancient India. It helps increase flexibility, reduce stress, and improve overall well-being.

  • Spinning

    Spinning is a high-intensity indoor cycling class that simulates outdoor riding. It's a great way to improve cardiovascular fitness and burn calories.

  • Pilates

    Pilates is a system of exercises designed to improve physical strength, flexibility, and posture, and enhance mental awareness.

  • Zumba

    Zumba is a dance fitness program that combines Latin and international music with dance moves. It's a fun and effective way to get a workout.
